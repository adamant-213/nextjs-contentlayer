## About Me

Hello! I&apos;m a developer with a passion for building thoughtful, practical software solutions. Over the years, I’ve worked on a wide range of projects using languages like **C#**, **Python**, and **Java**, and I enjoy tackling problems that require both creativity and technical skill.

This website serves as a central hub for my work — a place where I can document, reflect, and share projects and ideas that matter to me.

I created this site to:

- Showcase projects I’ve built and what I’ve learned from them

Whether you’re here to explore my work, learn something new, or connect professionally — welcome!
